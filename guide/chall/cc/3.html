Message: 43 6f 6e 67 72 61 74 75 6c 61 74 69 6f 6e 73 25 32 43 25 32 30 79 6f 75 25 32 30 68 61 76 65 ...

Theory

Seems like it's hexadecimal again, that's odd, so let's decode it to see if it might be hiding something else under the encoding.

Solution

Let's put it into CyberChef to decode from Hexadecimal:

recipe=From_Hex('Space')  # hex-to-text

- INPUT: 43 6f 6e 67 72 61 74 75 6c 61 74 69 6f 6e 73 25 32 43 25 32 30 79 6f 75 25 32 30 68 61 76 65 ...

- OUTPUT:
Congratulations%2C%20you%20have%20completed%20CyberChef%20challenge%20%233%21%0A%0AThis%20challenge%20explored%20URL%20percent%20encoding%2E%20To%20learn%20more%2C%20visit%20wikipedia%2Eorg%2Fwiki%2FPercent%2Dencoding%2E%0A%0AThe%20code%20for%20this%20challenge%20is%20959b09b9%2D267c%2D4585%2Dbc87%2D34aae2226e35%2E%0A%0AThe%20next%20challenge%20can%20be%20found%20at%20https%3A%2F%2Fpastebin%2Ecom%2Fqc0fb7Uw%2E

Oh, it's just URL encoded on top of the Hex, so we can just attach the URL Decode to get the original message:

recipe=URL_Decode(true)  # url-percent-decode

- INPUT: Congratulations%2C%20you%20have%20completed%20CyberChef%20challenge%20%233%21%0A%0AThis%20cha...

- OUTPUT:
