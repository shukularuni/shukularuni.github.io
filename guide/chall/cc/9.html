Theory

It's Base64 again, but we know from the previous challenge that it's because they're multiple layers of encryption. So, looking at the structure of the string, It's really confusing, like it's normal text but everything is swapped out, I think it might be a half caesar rotation, but I really don't know what this is. So let's try to decode it with rotation 13.

Solution

Let's put it into CyberChef to decode from Base64 to check what it really is:

recipe=ROT13(true,true,false,13)From_Base64('A-Za-z0-9+/=',true,false)  # rotation 13 and base64-to-text
