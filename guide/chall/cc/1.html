Theory

So the other day I was just playing around on CyberChef, when I found that in the terminal there is a sequence of what seems to be Hexadecimal characters. So let's decode it.

Solution

Let's put it into CyberChef to decode from Hex:

recipe=From_Hex('Space')  # hex-to-text
