Thanks go out to buttmonkey for creating the image :)

Theory

To get the solution, as the description says, we have to do some LSB magic (Least Significant Bit), this technique is usually used to uncover images below other images by just showing certain bits of each color, the challenge gives us a Hidden Hint to a download of Steganabara, but I tried it, and it seems really complicated. So instead I'll be using StegSolve (v1.3) by Caesum, which is so much easier to use, in Steganabara I needed to flip through the bits manually, whereas in StegSolve I just need to look through the images.

Solution

So let's download the image and put it into StegSolve:
