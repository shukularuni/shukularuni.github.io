Name: Training: Warchall - The Beginning
Tags: Realistic, Linux, Shell, Warchall
Score: 2
Description: You are now becoming a linux superhacker.

Create an SSH account with the form below.
Then enter the 6 solutions to level 0-5 separated by comma.
Example: bitwarrior,Solution1,Solution2,Solution3,Solution4,Solution5

Proudly presented by the The Warchall(tm) Staff
Thanks and shouts to xd-- for idea, motivation and inspiration!

Theory

To get the full answer with all 6 solutions, first we need to put a secure password on the form below the description, it doesn't necessarily have to be your account password. Then you have to run the command you get in your terminal, I don't know what we'll find in the SSH, so let's enter already.
