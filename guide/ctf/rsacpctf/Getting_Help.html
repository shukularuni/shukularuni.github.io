Getting Help

Name: Getting Help
Description: Let's make sure you can get help if you need it. Click on the "Stuck?" button above, and join the RSAC group for picoCTF. The flag is the picoCTF group name.
Type: General Skills
Challenge from: RSAC picoCTF 2025
Hints:
1. Sign up for a free account
2. Find the picoCTF group
3. The first four letters are "Sand".
4. If you are having trouble inputting a space, try copy-paste

Theory

According to the description, to get the flag we have to press the top-right button that says "Stuck? Get Help @ RSAC Groups", and that the flag should be the name of the group.

Solution

So first log in with your picoCTF RSAC credentials, and enter.

When you enter, you will see this:

And the name of it is (yes, the entire thing):

Sandbox: RSAC picoCTF

There we go! That's the flag.
