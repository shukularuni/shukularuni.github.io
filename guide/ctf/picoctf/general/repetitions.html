repetitions

Name: repetitions
Description: Can you make sense of this file? Download the file here.
Author: Theoneste Byagutangaza
Tags: Easy, General Skills, picoCTF 2023, base64
Challenge from: picoCTF 2023
Files: enc_flag
Hints:
1. Multiple decoding is always good.

Theory

According to the description, to get the flag we have to decode the text file they gave us, and I'm gonna go out on a limb and say it's the flag encoded in Base64 various times. This is because of the Base64 tag on the challenge info, and the title of the challenge being repetitions. So yeah, not much more to see in the challenge. If it really is Base64, I'm going to use an online decoder, not any fancy commands.
