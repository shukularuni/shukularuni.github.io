Hidden in plainsight

Name: Hidden in plainsight
Description: You're given a seemingly ordinary JPG image. Something is tucked away out of sight inside the file. Your task is to discover the hidden payload and extract the flag. Download the jpg image here.
Author: Yahaya Meddy
Tags: Easy, Forensics, picoMini by CMU-Africa, browser_webshell_solvable
Challenge from: picoMini by CMU-Africa
Files: img.jpg
Hints:
1. Download the jpg image and read its metadata

Theory

According to the description, to get the flag we have to look through an image to find something hidden within it. We'll use Exiftool like usual to check for any clues for how to get the flag. So let's download the image first.

Solution

Let's download the image and see what it has:

Oh yup, that is Base64 on the comment, so let's decode it:

BASE64 DECODE (Base64Decode.com)

INPUT: c3RlZ2hpZGU6Y0VGNmVuZHZjbVE9

OUTPUT: steghide:cEF6endvcmQ=

Oh it's StegHide with a Base64 password, well it's pretty simple to do, you just need StegHide installed and the password for it to work, so let's decode the password and uncover the secret behind this image:

BASE64 DECODE (Base64Decode.com)

INPUT: cEF6endvcmQ=

OUTPUT: pAzzword
shukularuni-picoctf@webshell:~$ steghide extract -sf img.jpg
Enter passphrase: pAzzword
wrote extracted data to "flag.txt".

shukularuni-picoctf@webshell:~$ cat flag.txt
picoCTF{h1dd3n_1n_1m4g3_e7f5b969}

There we go! That's the flag.
